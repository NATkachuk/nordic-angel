INTRODUCTION

Who are we?

Nordic Angels is an initiative looking to unite the benefactors in the Nordic startup ecosystem to increase both bandwidth and leverage. The definition of a Nordic Angel is any business angel who is contributing to the startup ecosystem as an investor, board member, advisor and/or in any other way invests time, money or effort to help the startup scene thrive.
