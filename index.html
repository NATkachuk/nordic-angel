INTRODUCTION

Who are we?

Nordic Angels is an initiative looking to unite the benefactors in the Nordic startup ecosystem to increase both bandwidth and leverage. The definition of a Nordic Angel is any business angel who is contributing to the startup ecosystem as an investor, board member, advisor and/or in any other way invests time, money or effort to help the startup scene thrive.

PRODUCT

We developing a digital eco-system for angel investors to streamline portfolio management, facilitate networking, and provide a platform for discovering investment opportunities. This digital ecosystem aims to simplify the angel investment process, making it more efficient and accessible for individuals to participate in and benefit from the startup ecosystem.
